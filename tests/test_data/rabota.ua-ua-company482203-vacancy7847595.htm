Middle Frontend Developer (Vue.js, AngularJS)

Навигатор, межрегиональная рекрутинговая компания

70000 3000$

Адрес: Львів
Контакт: Алла Мартынова
Телефон: 0674684363

German Software Company wants to set up a second location in Lviv in Ukraine at the beginning of next year to strengthen our team.

Your specific tasks would be to maintain company's software and to implement new functionality.

As a Frontend Developer we are looking for a passionate character who loves detailed work, such as the conception and extension of the existing user interface. You should have experience in designing, planning, and implementing new software projects. You should be a team player who is able to communicate with different departments and work closely with the design team. Additionally, you should be able to analyze and design REST interfaces.

Your Profile

  • Secure handling of web technologies like HTML5 / CSS3.
  • Extensive experience with frameworks such as Vue.js, AngularJS, React, jQuery, bootstrap and typically basic experience with the Laravel framework.
  • Experience in the implementation of mobile-first web applications.
  • Experience in continuous integration unit test / UI tests / acceptance tests.
  • Good knowledge of various tools (Gulp, Webpack, Babel, Browserify, NPM).
  • First experiences in agile software development (Scrum)
  • Safe handling of version control (Git).
  • You are a team player and feel comfortable within a workgroup.
  • You work independently, you are analytical and structured, you are organized and able to see the big picture even in stressful situations.
  • Good German and/or English.

Our proposal:

  • Good salaries that are still individually negotiable and it depends on the experience and skills of the candidates.
  • Teamwork and interesting project.

Please send us your application and CV. We are looking forward to your application.
